QUICK GUIDE - WIN11 LAB
=======================

Open:

E:\Lab_Win11\start_lab.cmd

Language: ENTER or 1 English, 2 Spanish, 3 French, 4 Romanian, 5 German

Most useful: option 1 HTML search, option 3 console copier, option 4 install.wim assistant, option 5 create USB, option 6 copy install.* to USB, option 7 copy boot.* to USB.

Work folders: images only boot.* and install.* (.wim/.esd/.swm); ISOs only .iso; Drivers; packages; offline.

Before mounting: offline must be empty; check dism /Get-MountedImageInfo; recover with Dism /Cleanup-Mountpoints.

During setup: Shift + F10 opens console; ncpa.cpl opens network adapters.
