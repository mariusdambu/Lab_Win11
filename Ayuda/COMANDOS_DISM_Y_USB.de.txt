KLARTEXT-BEFEHLE - WIN11 LAB
============================

Arbeitsordner:
E:\Lab_Win11\Trabajo

Windows PowerShell als Administrator oeffnen.


0) DISM-Hilfe und Status
------------------------
Dism /?

Dism /Get-MountedImageInfo

Dism /Cleanup-Mountpoints

dism /Get-MountedWimInfo

dism /Cleanup-Wim


1) Images anzeigen
------------------
Dism /Get-ImageInfo /ImageFile:"E:\Lab_Win11\Trabajo\images\install.wim"

Dism /Get-ImageInfo /ImageFile:"E:\Lab_Win11\Trabajo\images\install.wim" /Index:1 /English

Dism /Get-ImageInfo /ImageFile:"E:\Lab_Win11\Trabajo\images\boot.wim"


2) install.wim mounten und unmounten
------------------------------------
Dism /Mount-Image /ImageFile:"E:\Lab_Win11\Trabajo\images\install.wim" /Index:1 /MountDir:"E:\Lab_Win11\Trabajo\offline" /CheckIntegrity

Dism /Mount-Image /ImageFile:"E:\Lab_Win11\Trabajo\images\install.wim" /Name:"Windows 11 Pro" /MountDir:"E:\Lab_Win11\Trabajo\offline"

Dism /Commit-Image /MountDir:"E:\Lab_Win11\Trabajo\offline" /CheckIntegrity

Dism /Unmount-Image /MountDir:"E:\Lab_Win11\Trabajo\offline" /Commit /CheckIntegrity

Dism /Unmount-Image /MountDir:"E:\Lab_Win11\Trabajo\offline" /Discard


3) Treiber
----------
Dism /Image:"E:\Lab_Win11\Trabajo\offline" /Add-Driver /Driver:"E:\Lab_Win11\Trabajo\Drivers" /Recurse /ForceUnsigned

Dism /Image:"E:\Lab_Win11\Trabajo\offline" /Get-Drivers /Format:Table

Dism /Image:"E:\Lab_Win11\Trabajo\offline" /Get-Drivers /All /Format:Table

Dism /Image:"E:\Lab_Win11\Trabajo\offline" /Get-DriverInfo /Driver:oem1.inf

Dism /Online /Export-Driver /Destination:"E:\Lab_Win11\Trabajo\ExportedDrivers"


4) Packages und Features
------------------------
Dism /Image:"E:\Lab_Win11\Trabajo\offline" /Add-Package /PackagePath:"E:\Lab_Win11\Trabajo\packages"

Dism /Image:"E:\Lab_Win11\Trabajo\offline" /Get-Packages /Format:Table

Dism /Image:"E:\Lab_Win11\Trabajo\offline" /Get-PackageInfo /PackagePath:"E:\Lab_Win11\Trabajo\packages\NOMBRE.cab"

Dism /Image:"E:\Lab_Win11\Trabajo\offline" /Get-Features /Format:Table

Dism /Image:"E:\Lab_Win11\Trabajo\offline" /Enable-Feature /FeatureName:NetFx3 /All /Source:"X:\sources\sxs" /LimitAccess


5) Bereinigung und Reparatur
----------------------------
Dism /Image:"E:\Lab_Win11\Trabajo\offline" /Cleanup-Image /CheckHealth

Dism /Image:"E:\Lab_Win11\Trabajo\offline" /Cleanup-Image /ScanHealth

Dism /Image:"E:\Lab_Win11\Trabajo\offline" /Cleanup-Image /RestoreHealth

Dism /Image:"E:\Lab_Win11\Trabajo\offline" /Cleanup-Image /RestoreHealth /Source:"X:\sources\install.wim" /LimitAccess

Dism /Image:"E:\Lab_Win11\Trabajo\offline" /Cleanup-Image /StartComponentCleanup

Dism /Image:"E:\Lab_Win11\Trabajo\offline" /Cleanup-Image /StartComponentCleanup /ResetBase


6) WIM, ESD und SWM
-------------------
Dism /Export-Image /SourceImageFile:"E:\Lab_Win11\Trabajo\images\install.esd" /SourceIndex:1 /DestinationImageFile:"E:\Lab_Win11\Trabajo\images\install.wim" /Compress:max /CheckIntegrity

Dism /Export-Image /SourceImageFile:"E:\Lab_Win11\Trabajo\images\install.wim" /SourceIndex:1 /DestinationImageFile:"E:\Lab_Win11\Trabajo\images\install.esd" /Compress:recovery /CheckIntegrity

Dism /Export-Image /SourceImageFile:"E:\Lab_Win11\Trabajo\images\install.wim" /SourceIndex:1 /DestinationImageFile:"E:\Lab_Win11\Trabajo\images\install_optimizado.wim" /Compress:max /CheckIntegrity

Dism /Split-Image /ImageFile:"E:\Lab_Win11\Trabajo\images\install.wim" /SWMFile:"E:\Lab_Win11\Trabajo\images\install.swm" /FileSize:4000


7) boot.wim, WinPE und WinRE
----------------------------
DISM /Mount-Wim /WimFile:"E:\Lab_Win11\Trabajo\images\boot.wim" /Index:2 /MountDir:"E:\Lab_Win11\Trabajo\offline"

Dism /Image:"E:\Lab_Win11\Trabajo\offline" /Add-Driver /Driver:"E:\Lab_Win11\Trabajo\Drivers" /Recurse /ForceUnsigned

Dism /Unmount-Image /MountDir:"E:\Lab_Win11\Trabajo\offline" /Commit

reagentc /info

Dism /Mount-Wim /WimFile:"R:\Recovery\WindowsRE\winre.wim" /Index:1 /MountDir:"E:\Lab_Win11\Trabajo\offline"


8) USB und ISO
--------------
Get-Disk | Sort-Object Number | Format-Table Number,FriendlyName,BusType,Size,PartitionStyle,IsBoot,IsSystem -AutoSize

Get-Volume | Sort-Object DriveLetter | Format-Table DriveLetter,FileSystemLabel,FileSystem,DriveType,SizeRemaining,Size -AutoSize

Mount-DiskImage -ImagePath "E:\Lab_Win11\Trabajo\ISOs\Win11.iso"

Dismount-DiskImage -ImagePath "E:\Lab_Win11\Trabajo\ISOs\Win11.iso"

robocopy X:\ Z:\ /E /COPY:DAT /DCOPY:DAT /R:2 /W:2 /XD sources

New-Item -ItemType Directory -Path "Z:\sources" -Force

Copy-Item "E:\Lab_Win11\Trabajo\images\boot.wim" "Z:\sources\boot.wim" -Force

robocopy X:\ Y:\ /E /COPY:DAT /DCOPY:DAT /R:2 /W:2 /XF boot.wim boot.esd boot.swm install.wim install.esd install*.swm

Copy-Item "E:\Lab_Win11\Trabajo\images\install.wim" "Y:\sources\install.wim" -Force
